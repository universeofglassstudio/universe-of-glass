About Universe of Glass Studio

Universe of Glass Studio was born from Gabriela’s passion for stained glass art, discovered in 2022 and turned into a dream come true in 2025. Today, each handmade piece is created to bring the beauty of glass into people’s lives through light, color, and forms full of personality.
